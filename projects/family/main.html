Meet the Forger Family

Loid Forger

Loid Forger, also known as Twilight, is a highly skilled spy working for an organization, he takes on the identity of a psychiatrist to maintain his cover. Driven by a mission to maintain peace between rival nations, Loid adopts Anya and later marries Yor, unaware of their own hidden identities. He is dedicated, resourceful, and often struggles to balance his secret life with his newfound role as a father and husband. Despite his serious demeanor, Loid shows a caring side, especially toward Anya, highlighting his deepening bond with his unconventional family.

Yor Forger

Yor Forger, also known as Thorn Princess. By day, she works as a city hall employee, but by night, she is a skilled assassin known for her lethal combat skills. Yor initially marries Loid to maintain her cover while hiding her true profession. Despite her dangerous job, she has a kind-hearted and gentle nature, often struggling with feelings of inadequacy as a mother to Anya. Yor's fierce loyalty to her family and her desire to protect them add depth to her character, making her a captivating blend of warmth and strength in a world filled with espionage and danger.

Anya Forger

Anya Forger is a charming and quirky young girl. As the telepathic daughter of a spy and an assassin, Anya has a unique ability to read minds, which she often uses to navigate her unconventional family life and the challenges of her school.
